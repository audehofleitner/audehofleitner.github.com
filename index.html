I am a PhD candidate in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at the University of California, Berkley. I work with Professor Alex Bayen and Professor Pieter Abbeel on the Mobile Millennium project in the Intelligent Systems, Control, and Robotics Area (CIR). My research interests span statistical models and optimization, control and estimation theory, with applications to traffic estimation and pattern learning from GPS probe vehicle.
